Breaking Bad didn't become a phenomenon overnight. Its first season was cut short to just seven episodes by the 2007 writers' strike, and ratings were modest. But word of mouth spread, and Netflix made all previous seasons available between airings — introducing millions of new fans. By the time the finale aired in 2013, over ten million viewers tuned in live. The show won sixteen Emmy Awards, including Outstanding Drama Series, and is now widely considered one of the greatest television series ever made.
