Getting Breaking Bad made was far from easy. Major networks including HBO, FX, and TNT all passed on the project. It was AMC — a channel better known for airing classic films — that took the leap, making it their very first original scripted drama. The casting of Bryan Cranston as Walter White raised eyebrows; audiences knew him as the bumbling dad from Malcolm in the Middle. But Gilligan trusted him completely. The pilot debuted on January 20th, 2008, filmed entirely on location in Albuquerque, New Mexico.
