# Breaking Bad: The Idea

- Creator: **Vince Gilligan**, writer on *The X-Files*
- Conceived in **2004** during a phone call with a colleague
- The pitch: *"What if Mr. Chips became Scarface?"*
- Inspired by a dark joke about cooking meth in an RV to make money
- Gilligan wanted to explore a character transformation from good to evil
