# Breaking Bad: Premiere & Legacy

- Slow build: modest ratings in **Season 1** (7 episodes due to writers' strike)
- Word-of-mouth and **Netflix** drove massive audience growth
- By Season 5, **10.3 million viewers** watched the finale live
- Won **16 Emmy Awards**, including Outstanding Drama Series
- Widely regarded as one of the **greatest TV shows ever made**
