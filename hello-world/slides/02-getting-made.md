# Breaking Bad: Getting Made

- Gilligan pitched the show to **HBO, FX, and TNT** — all passed
- **AMC** picked it up in **2007**, their first scripted original drama
- Bryan Cranston cast as Walter White — known mainly for *Malcolm in the Middle*
- Shot on location in **Albuquerque, New Mexico**
- Pilot aired **January 20, 2008**
