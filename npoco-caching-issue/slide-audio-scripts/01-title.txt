For years, there has been a bug in this codebase that nobody fully understood. It caused widespread failures during deployments, left users unable to operate on their open requests, and was growing more frequent. The workaround was simple: redeploy the affected server. The root cause was not. Today we'll trace the problem from its surface symptoms all the way down to a race condition in NPoco's IL generator — and show that the fix, once found, was just five lines.
