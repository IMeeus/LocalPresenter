For years, there has been a bug in this codebase that nobody fully understood. It caused failures when users tried to query or act on their open requests — seemingly at random, but always after a fresh deployment. It left users unable to operate on their requests, and was growing more frequent. The workaround was simple: redeploy the affected server. The root cause was not. Today we'll trace the problem from its surface symptoms all the way down to a race condition in NPoco's intermediate language generator — and show that the fix, once found, was just five lines.
